Fenwick Road

Article 4 Direction  ·  Last updated: 18 March 2026  ·  Active

Covers a residential area in the north of the district where a high concentration of Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs) has led to local amenity concerns. This direction was made to allow the council to assess applications for HMOs on their individual merits.

If you are unsure whether this direction applies to your property or proposed works, contact the planning team before beginning any alterations.

Direction details

ReferenceWBDC-A4D-002
Area nameFenwick Road
Date confirmed22 July 2021
Date in effect22 August 2021
StatusActive
LegislationTown and Country Planning Act 1990, Article 4 of the Town and Country Planning (General Permitted Development) (England) Order 2015

Permitted development rights removed

Within the Fenwick Road Article 4 Direction area, planning permission is required for the following works that would otherwise be permitted development:

  • Change of use to Houses in Multiple Occupation (Class C4)
  • Sub-division of dwelling houses into two or more separate dwellings
