Local Plans

This page provides access to West Brackenfield District Council's current and emerging local plans, along with the Local Plan Timetable and open data downloads.

Local Plans

West Brackenfield Local Plan 2026–2042 (emerging)
Reg 19 published
Reference: WBDC-LP2042 Reg 19 published: 10 November 2025 Consultation closed: 9 January 2026 Period: 2026–2042
Consultation closed — submission to the Planning Inspectorate expected April 2026. Representations received during the Regulation 19 consultation are being collated prior to submission. The emerging plan is a material consideration in planning decisions.

The new Local Plan sets out a spatial strategy for the district to 2042, including updated housing and employment land requirements in line with national planning policy. It will replace the adopted 2017 plan on adoption.

West Brackenfield Local Plan 2014–2036
Adopted
Reference: WBDC-LP2036 Adopted: 22 June 2017 Period: 2014–2036 Area: West Brackenfield District
Note: A new Local Plan is at Regulation 19 stage and will replace this plan on adoption. In the meantime this plan remains the primary development plan for the district, though some policies may carry limited weight given its age.

The adopted Local Plan sets out the spatial strategy and land use policies for West Brackenfield. Adopted in June 2017 following independent examination, the plan allocates sites for housing, employment, retail, and community uses across the district.

Fully superseded on adoption of the 2014–2036 plan. Retained here for reference only; carries no planning weight.
